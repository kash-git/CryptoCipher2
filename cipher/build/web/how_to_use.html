How to use

  • On home page enter the text you want to encrypt in the text area or upload a text document.
  • Click on encrypt button to encrypt the text.
  • Your encrypted text will appear on the next page.
  • From there you can either download the encrypted text or decrypt it.
  • Click on decrypt button to decrypt the text.
  • The next page will show the decrypted text.
  • Click on encrypt to encrypt another text.
  • Or you can download the decrypted text.
